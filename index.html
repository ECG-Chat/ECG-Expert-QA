The ECG-Expert-QA Dataset is a comprehensive collection of electrocardiogram (ECG) data paired with expert-level questions and answers. This dataset is designed to advance the field of medical AI, particularly in the domain of cardiology.

Key features of our dataset include:

  • High-quality ECG recordings from diverse patient populations
  • Expert-curated questions covering a wide range of cardiac conditions
  • Detailed answers provided by experienced cardiologists
  • Annotations for abnormalities and diagnostic insights

Our dataset aims to bridge the gap between machine learning models and expert-level ECG interpretation, paving the way for more accurate and reliable AI-assisted cardiac diagnostics.
